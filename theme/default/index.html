How to Play

Three quick steps to get going in Relding.

1

Move

Walk with W A S D or the arrow keys. You can also left-click the ground to walk there.

2

Explore

Walk through doors and bridges (change points) to travel between towns, houses and forests.

3

Interact

Right-click or press TAB to target an NPC or player, then use the on-screen action buttons.
