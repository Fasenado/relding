Relding Documentation

Everything you need to start playing Relding — an open-world pixel life simulation you can run offline today, with Solana wallet sign-in and a fully on-chain future.

Overview

Relding is a real-time, multiplayer pixel-art life simulation. You create a character, explore a connected open world of towns, houses and forests, meet other players and NPCs, collect items, and grow over time. The game runs on an authoritative server, so your progress is saved and the world stays consistent for everyone.

Getting started

  1. Open the game and choose how to sign in: Connect Phantom (Solana wallet) or Play as Guest.
  2. Pick a starting location and create your character (choose a name).
  3. You spawn into the world — start walking around and exploring!
Guest vs. Wallet

A guest account is great for a quick try, but it is temporary. Signing in with a wallet ties your characters and progress to your Solana address, so you keep them across sessions and devices.

Wallet login Solana

Relding uses the Phantom wallet for sign-in. Your wallet is your identity — the same wallet always logs into the same account.

How it works

  1. Install the Phantom browser extension (or app).
  2. Click Connect Phantom on the start screen.
  3. Approve the connection, then sign the message Phantom shows you. This signature proves you own the wallet — it never moves any funds and costs nothing.
  4. You're in. Your wallet address becomes your account.

Note: signing the message is free and does not send a transaction. Relding will never ask you to approve a transfer just to log in.

Exploring the world

The world is made of connected scenes — towns, houses, forests and more. To travel between them, walk your character through a change point: a doorway, a bridge, or a path edge. The next scene loads automatically and other players there will see you arrive.

  • Click-to-move: left-click anywhere walkable and your character paths there.
  • Doors & bridges: step onto them to change scene.
  • Minimap: use it to keep your bearings in larger areas.

NPCs & interaction

Towns are full of NPCs — merchants, healers, trainers and quest givers. Approach one and target it (right-click or TAB); a glowing reticle shows your current target. Then interact to open a dialog, trade, or accept a task.

  • Merchants buy and sell items.
  • Healers restore your stats.
  • Quest givers offer objectives and rewards.

Characters & stats

You can own multiple characters and pick one each time you play. Every character has a level and core stats:

Your HP/MP bars are shown in the player panel during play.

Inventory & gear

Collect items as you explore and complete tasks. Open the Inventory to view what you own, and the Equipment panel to equip gear into slots. Equipped items can change how your character looks and performs.

  • Inventory: all your items and consumables.
  • Equipment: drag items into slots to wear them.
  • Trade: exchange items with merchants or other players.

Social & chat

Relding is multiplayer — you'll see other players moving around the same scenes in real time.

  • Chat: send public messages; overhead bubbles appear above players.
  • Teams: group up with other players to share progress.
  • Clans: join a larger community with shared goals.

On-chain roadmap Coming soon

Relding is built offline-first, then connected to Solana. Wallet sign-in is the first step. Planned web3 features:

  • Asset ownership: characters, items and property as NFTs in your wallet.
  • True trading: peer-to-peer trades settled on-chain.
  • Player-owned property: houses and land you actually own.

Until these ship, signing in only proves wallet ownership — nothing is minted and no funds are touched.

FAQ

Do I need a wallet to play?

No. You can Play as Guest instantly. A wallet is recommended so your progress is permanent.

Does logging in cost anything?

No. You only sign a message to prove ownership of your wallet — there is no transaction or fee.

Can I play on mobile?

The game is designed for desktop with keyboard and mouse. Mobile support may come later.

Where is my progress stored?

On the game server, tied to your account (wallet or guest). Guest accounts are temporary.
